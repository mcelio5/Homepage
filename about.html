About the Sapperton Pensioners Hall

The Sapperton Pensioners Hall, located at 318 Keary Street in New Westminster has been serving the community since 1962. It's run by The Sapperton Old Age Pensioners Association(SOAPA). SOAPA is a Registered Charity and Not-For-Profit Organization. We organize all events and activities at the hall.

Our Mandate

The Sapperton Old Age Pensioners Association aims to provide a happier, healthier, and more fulfilling life experience for all seniors in our diverse community. We offer and support various activities, socials, informative health, wellness and financial forums, all taking place in a safe, and welcoming environment.

The association has a 7,000 sq ft., well maintained "vintage" hall that is used for a number of community events throughout the year. These community groups and organizations are very diverse and range from small gatherings, weddings, family receptions to large meetings and socials. These include but are not limited to the City of New Westminster, Greater Vancouver Woodturners Guild, Royal City Swing, ECCW Wrestling and Beacon Unitarian Church.
